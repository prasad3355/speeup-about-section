The Origin

Why Speeup Started

The Problem We Saw

In Khallikote, we watched our parents struggle to get fresh groceries after work hours. Stores closed early. Trips took time. Options were limited. Meanwhile, metros enjoyed 10-minute delivery. This gap bothered us.

The Experiment

We started small — partnering with 5 local shops, one delivery bike, and a WhatsApp group. Within weeks, families were ordering daily. They loved the speed. They trusted the faces delivering their groceries.

The Belief

We realized — Bharat doesn't need a copy of metro models. It needs solutions designed for its unique culture, density, and aspirations. Speeup was born to fill that void, village by village, city by city.

Today & Beyond

From Khallikote to Berhampur, Bhubaneswar to beyond — we're expanding with the same ethos: hyperlocal roots, national ambition, and technology that puts people first.
